Bughouse Clock App

Intro

Using Xcode and the Apple developer tool SwiftUI, I created an app chess clock app for the bughouse chess variant.

Summary

Bughouse is a chess variant that allows 2v2 team play. This requires two simultanesou clocks to be running in order to keep the game timely, and lively. This app start two synchronous clocks and is specifcally designed for the bughouse chess variant.

In order to develop this app I used SwiftUI, Xcode, Figma, UI design, and Apple developer tools.

Picture on the left shows a screenshot of the app from my phone.

Future Iterations/Improvements

Future updates to this app will more animations as the game ends, score tracker to see which teams have won more games, and a move counter to keep track of the total amount of moves.
